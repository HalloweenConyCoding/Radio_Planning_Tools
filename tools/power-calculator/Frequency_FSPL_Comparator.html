Formula used:
FSPL (dB) = 32.44 + 20log10(distance in km) + 20log10(frequency in MHz)
Total Loss (dB) = FSPL + Extra Attenuation
Balanced Power 2 = Power 1 - (Loss 2 - Loss 1)
Balanced Power 1 = Power 2 - (Loss 1 - Loss 2)
